About us

Our Story

Welcome to Pendle Burton Restaurant nestled in the heart of Larnaca, along the enchanting shores of the Mediterranean island. Our culinary journey started with a vision to create a dining experience that celebrates the bounty of the sea and the freshness of local ingredients. Indulge in our flavorsome dishes crafted with care and enjoy the tranquil ambiance overlooking the azure waters. Come savor the essence of coastal living with us."Join us to experience coastal living at its finest in our seaside restaurant, where every dish tells a story of the Mediterranean."
